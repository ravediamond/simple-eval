Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how EvalNow handles your data.

Privacy-First Approach: EvalNow is designed with privacy at its core. We don't store your data, track your usage, or collect personal information.

Data Processing

When you upload a file to EvalNow:

  • Temporary Processing: Your file is processed in memory only
  • AI Evaluation: Content is sent to Google's Gemini API for evaluation
  • Immediate Deletion: All data is discarded immediately after processing
  • No Storage: We don't save, cache, or store your files or evaluation results

What We Don't Collect

EvalNow does not collect:

  • Personal information or user accounts
  • File contents or evaluation results
  • Usage analytics or tracking cookies
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Behavioral or preference data

Third-Party Services

EvalNow uses Google's Gemini AI service for evaluation. Your question, reference, and answer data is sent to Google's servers for processing according to Google Cloud Terms of Service and their Privacy Policy.

Security Measures

We implement several security measures:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted
  • No Data Persistence: Eliminates data breach risks
  • Minimal Data Exposure: Only evaluation-relevant data is processed
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on secure cloud platforms

Your Control

Since we don't store any data, you maintain complete control:

  • No need to request data deletion - nothing is stored
  • No account creation or personal data required
  • Use the service anonymously
  • Stop using the service at any time with no data trail

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or EvalNow's data practices, you can contact us through our About page.
